Who We Are

About Us

The Photographic Society of Richmond College — a community built on passion, vision, and the art of capturing the world.

The Photographic Society of Richmond College is one of the most active and celebrated student societies on campus. Founded with the belief that photography is more than just a technical skill — it is a language, a means of expression, and a way of seeing the world differently.

We bring together students from all disciplines who share a passion for visual storytelling. Through workshops, field shoots, exhibitions, and competitions, we nurture talent at every level — from curious beginners to seasoned photographers.

Our society has consistently produced award-winning photographers and continues to be a vibrant hub of creative energy at Richmond College.

The Event

About Luminate

Luminate '26, organized by the Richmond College Photographic Society, returns after 9 years as a celebration of creativity, vision, and storytelling through photography.

Each year, Luminate sets a unique theme that challenges participants to interpret the world through a fresh lens. The event features a competitive photography showcase, judged by industry professionals, alongside workshops, panel discussions, and an open gallery.

Luminate is not just a competition — it is a festival of photography that inspires, educates, and connects the creative community of Sri Lanka.

Photography Competition

Submit your best shots across multiple categories and compete for top honours judged by professional photographers.

Exhibition

Shortlisted works will be displayed in a curated gallery open to the public throughout the event.

Workshop

Hands-on sessions and panel discussions with industry professionals covering technique, editing, and visual storytelling.

Awards Ceremony

A closing ceremony celebrating winners across all categories with trophies, certificates, and special recognitions.

Online Photography Competition

Participants can submit photographs under three categories: Colour, Monochrome, and Mobile. All submissions will be evaluated by three professional judges.

On-Site Marathon

An exciting live competition with two categories: Event Photography and Model Photography.

Quiz Paper

A 60-minute written paper covering photography history, terminology, famous photographers, general knowledge, and camera equipment.

Quiz Presentation

Teams are given a random photography-related topic and have 30 minutes to prepare a 5-minute presentation.

Competition

Competition Categories

Three distinct categories — each a different way of seeing the world.

01
Colour
Photographs submitted in full colour. Any subject matter — showcasing the richness and depth that colour brings to a frame.
02
Monochrome
Black & white photography. Stripped of colour, every composition must stand on light, shadow, texture, and form alone.
03
Mobile
Shot entirely on a mobile device. A celebration of accessible photography and the power of the camera in your pocket.
Rules & Regulations

Competition Guidelines

Submission Guidelines

  • Maximum 04 photographs per category
  • Submit photos in JPEG format (.jpg/.jpeg), max file size: 10MB
  • No watermarks, date stamps, names, or logos on images
  • Basic editing (cropping, exposure, contrast, masking) allowed
  • AI images, manipulation images, or over-editing will be disqualified without announcement
  • Previously awarded entries will be disqualified
  • Entries must be original. Participants may be asked to provide the RAW or unedited file upon request
  • Resize your photograph to 1920 × 1080 resolution
